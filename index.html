About

Hey peope!

You've just stumbled onto Learning to Cook, a recipe log for my learnings.

Since I've left my family's nest and started living idependently, I've craved the food that was so deliciously crafted by my mother, grandmothers and other women in my immediate and extended family.

Over the years, I've collected and taken note of those recipes from the experts. I've tried to replicate them with varying levels of success. And although I'm still not an intuitive cook (I've got measurements for seasonings) - I thought it might be a good idea to share my journey as I learn.

Learning to Cook is my attempt at documenting this journey. In the process I'm also creating video documents - primarily for my future self.

Although the recipes are here to stay, but as I experiment and learn new techniques - I will update the recipes from time to time. This blog is the place to check back on - even though the videos are not updated.
