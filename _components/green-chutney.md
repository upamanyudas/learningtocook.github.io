ingredients:
- 50 g fresh coriander
- 4 pcs green chilli
- 20 ml lemon juice
- 5 g of salt
- 15 g of sugar
- 50 ml water

directions:
- Add all the ingredients to a blender jar.
- Blend everything to a smooth paste.
- Store and use as required.

---

A simple chutney for Sandwiches, Chaat and Indian snacks. It is the perfect side to snacks & appetizers. Not to forget it takes chaats, wraps, rolls, sandwiches, pakoras, dhokla & cutlets to a next level of deliciousness.
