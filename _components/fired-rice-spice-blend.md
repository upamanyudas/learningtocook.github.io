title:  "Fried Rice Spice Blend"
image: fried-rice-spice-blend.jpg
# imagecredit:

serves: 46 g

ingredients:
- 1/2 tsp peppercorns (crushed)
- 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
- 16 g salt
- 26 g sugar

directions:
- Add all the ingredients to a jar.
- Shake well.
- Use once or store for later.
